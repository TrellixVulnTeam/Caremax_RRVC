Welcome to Caremax



Caremax is a hospital management system designed to simplify patient management and tracking.
Its developed on a secure platform that only allows registered and authorized users to gain access to it after successful login.

How it works

Our Services



Caremax offers the following functionalities:

  1. Registration of new Patients.
  2. Editing of already registered patient's bio-data
  3. Deleting of registered patients
  4. Storing, editing, deleting and displaying patients' prescription history
  5. Records patients visitations
  6. It tracks Patients bills and payment history over time
  7. It allows patients to schedule an appointment ahead of time
  8. It track all tests carriedout on a particular patient
  9. Caremax tracks the treatment give to a patient on a particular visit
